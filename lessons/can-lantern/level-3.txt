Can the Lantern Fly?

There is a paper lantern on the desk.

"Can it fly?" Yui asks.

"No," Ken says. "It is paper."

The window opens. The lantern moves a little.

Mika smiles. "Can the wind help?"

They tie a light string to the lantern. The wind comes in again. The lantern rises and touches the curtain.

Taro jumps. "It can fly!"

Then the string pulls it back to the desk.

Yui writes a small card and puts it inside the lantern.

The card says, "Can your idea fly?"

Ken nods. "Maybe yes, with a little wind."

Yui puts the lantern by the window after class.

It moves each time the wind comes in.

Taro whispers, "It is practicing."
