Don't Be Late

"Don't be late," Mika says.

Ken has the music for the class dance. The show starts at two.

At one fifty, Ken is not in the hall.

Taro runs to the gate. Ken is there with a first-year student. The student's bag is open, and books are on the ground.

"I helped him," Ken says.

Taro takes the music. Ken helps with the books.

They run to the hall together.

Mika starts the music at two.

After the dance, she says, "You were almost late."

Ken says, "I know."

The first-year student smiles. "But he was not late for me."

Mika looks at Ken and then at the student.

"Next time, call us," she says.

Ken nods. "Then no one will be late."
