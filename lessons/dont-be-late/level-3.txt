Don't Be Late

"Don't be late," Saki says.

Kaito has the music for the class dance. The show starts at two.

At one fifty, Kaito is not in the hall.

Ren runs to the gate. Kaito is there with a first-year student. The student's bag is open, and books are on the ground.

"I helped him," Kaito says.

Ren takes the music. Kaito helps with the books.

They run to the hall together.

Saki starts the music at two.

After the dance, she says, "You were almost late."

Kaito says, "I know."

The first-year student smiles. "But he was not late for me."

Saki looks at Kaito and then at the student.

"Next time, call us," she says.

Kaito nods. "Then no one will be late."
