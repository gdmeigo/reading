The Red Pin

You are in the room. He is here. She is here. It is here, too.

Ken sees a red pin on the desk. "It is not my pin," he says.

Miki looks at it. "It is his pin," she says. But Taro says, "No. It is not my pin."

The pin is small, but it has a paper flag on it. The flag says, "Open the blue box."

Ken opens the blue box. There is a small cake in it.

"It is for you," Miki says.

Ken looks at Taro. Taro smiles. "It is her idea," he says.

Miki smiles, too. "Happy birthday."

Ken sees the red pin again. It is not a mystery now. It is a tiny flag for a happy secret.
