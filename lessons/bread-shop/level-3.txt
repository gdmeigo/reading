The Quiet Bread Shop

Daichi and Emi walked by a bread shop.
The shop was quiet.
No one was in it.

There were many rolls on the table.
An old man was behind the table.
He looked sad.

Emi saw a sign on the door.
It said, "Closed."

"But the door is open," said Daichi.
Emi turned the sign.
Now it said, "Open."

Soon, a woman came in.
Then two boys came in.
They bought rolls.

The old man smiled.
"Oh," he said. "That was the problem."
