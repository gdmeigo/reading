Where the Roof Door Opens

The school roof door was always locked.
Everyone knew that.

But on Friday, Ken found a wet footprint by the stairs.
It went up, not down.

"Where does it go?" Mika asked.

Ken pointed to the roof door.
There was no key in the lock, but the door was open a little.

Taro came with a flashlight.
"Maybe someone is hiding there."

They opened the door and went up.
The roof was empty.
Only one blue umbrella was near the fence.

Mika looked at the umbrella.
"This is dry. The footprint was wet."

Ken saw a thin string on the floor.
The string went from the umbrella to a small box under the water tank.

Inside the box was a notebook.
The first page said, "Where is the rain?"

Taro looked at the clear sky.
"That is a strange question."

Ken turned the page.
There was a drawing of the roof.
One corner was marked with a star.

They walked to the star.
Under a loose tile, Mika found a small glass bottle.
Inside it was water and a paper flower.

Mr. Sato came up the stairs.
"You found it," he said.

He explained that the science club had made a rain recorder last year.
The bottle opened when rain fell, but no one remembered where it was.

Ken looked at the footprint again.
It was not from a person.
Water had run from the tank and made the mark.

Mika smiled.
"So the clue was not who. It was where."
