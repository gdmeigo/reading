Where Is the Bell?

"Where is the bell?" Ms. Sato asks.

The small bell is not on the teacher's desk. It is not in the box. It is not under the chair.

Ken hears a soft sound.

Ring.

"Where is that sound?" Yui asks.

Ring.

The sound comes from the window. Ken opens it. There is no bell.

Then a cat walks by the window. A ribbon is on its neck. The bell is on the ribbon.

"There it is!" Ken says.

The cat looks at the class. It rings the bell again.

Ms. Sato smiles. "Today, the cat is the teacher."

The cat sits by the door. Ken writes on the board: "Where is the bell? It is on the cat."
