One Pen and One Pen

1

This is Aki.

This is Meg.

This is Aki's pen.

This is Meg's pen.

Aki puts one pen in the water.

Meg puts one pen on the book.

Aki sees the pen in the water.

Meg sees the pen on the book.

2

Aki sees the water.

He sees one pen in the water.

The pen is in the water.

Meg sees the book.

She sees one pen on the book.

The pen is on the book.

One pen is in the water.

One pen is on the book.

3

Aki takes this pen.

Aki puts this pen on the box.

Meg takes that pen.

Meg puts that pen in the water.

Aki sees this pen on the box.

Meg sees that pen in the water.

4

This pen is not in the water.

This pen is on the box.

That pen is not on the book.

That pen is in the water.

Aki sees this pen.

Meg sees that pen.

One pen is here. One pen is there.
