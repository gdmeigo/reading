The Book in the Box

EP Book1 to 41-10
GDM Reading Sample 01
1

Aki has a book.

Meg has a pen.

Aki says, "This is my book."

Meg says, "Yes. That is your book."

Aki puts the book in the box.

Meg puts the pen on the box.

2

Aki does not see the book.

He sees the pen.

Aki says, "Where is my book?"

Meg sees the box.

She says, "It is in the box."

Aki says, "No. I do not see it."

3

Then the box goes.

Aki sees the box.

Meg sees the box.

They say, "What?"

A man is in the box.

The man has Aki's book.

Aki says, "That is my book!"

4

The man gives Aki the book.

Aki gives Meg the pen.

Meg says, "This is not my pen."

Aki says, "What?"

Meg says, "It is your pen."

Aki sees the pen.

Aki says, "I see."

Meg and Aki go together.
