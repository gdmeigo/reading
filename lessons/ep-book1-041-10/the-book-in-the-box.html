The Same Pen

1

This is Ken.

This is Yui.

This is one long pen.

Ken puts the pen on the table.

Yui puts a glass of water on the table.

Ken sees the pen.

The pen looks long.

It is not short.

2

Ken puts the same pen in the water.

He looks at the side of the glass.

The pen looks short.

Yui looks at the pen.

She sees a short pen, too.

But it is the same pen.

It is the same long pen.

3

Yui takes the pen out of the water.

Ken sees the pen again.

It looks long again.

Ken puts the same pen back in the water.

It looks short again.

The pen is not short.

It only looks short in the water.

4

Ken puts half of the pen in the water.

Half of the pen is out of the water.

The pen looks broken at the water line.

Yui takes the pen out.

The pen is not broken.

The same long pen can look short and broken in water.
