The Book in the Box

1

This is Aki's book.

This is Meg's pen.

Aki puts the book in the box.

Meg puts the pen on the box.

2

Aki does not see the book.

He sees the pen.

Meg sees the box.

The book is not in the box.

3

A man is in the box.

Aki's book is with the man.

Aki sees the man.

Meg sees the man.

That is Aki's book.

4

The man gave Aki the book.

The man gave Meg the pen.

Aki sees the book.

Meg sees the pen.

The man is in the box again.
