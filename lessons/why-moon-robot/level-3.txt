Why the Moon Robot Stops

The science club has a moon robot.
It is small, but it can walk over stones.

Ken puts a white stone on the floor.
The robot walks to it.

Then it stops.

"Why does it stop?" Mika asks.

Taro looks at the robot's eye.
The eye is looking at the window.

Outside, the real moon is in the sky.
The robot sends a blue light to the moon.

"Maybe it thinks the moon is home," Ken says.

Mika puts a paper moon on the desk.
The robot walks again.

It stops by the paper moon and makes a happy sound.

Taro laughs. "Now it has two homes."
