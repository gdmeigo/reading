Voices in the Wall

Ken hears a small voice near the back wall.

"Help," it says.

Riku steps back. "The wall can speak."

Mika puts her ear near the wall. "The voice was made by something small."

Ken looks behind the map. There is a round hole.

Inside the hole is a toy recorder. It says, "Help," again.

"Who put it there?" Riku asks.

Mika sees dust on the map. One corner is clean. "The map was moved by someone today."

Mr. Sato comes in with a ladder. "I did it," he says. "I was checking the old speaker line."

Ken presses the recorder. It says, "Thank you."

Riku smiles. "Now the wall is polite."
