Lost Library Card

Emi finds a library card on the stairs.

The name is hard to read because the card is wet.

Daichi says, "We can visit the library and ask."

At the library, Yuna is looking under a table.

"I feel bad," she says. "My brother lost his card, and he thinks he cannot read today."

Emi shows her the wet card.

Yuna smiles. "That is it."

They listen to the librarian. The card still works.

Yuna's brother comes in slowly. Emi gives him the card.

He holds it like a ticket.

"Can I read now?" he asks.

Daichi points to the shelves. "You can travel anywhere from here."
