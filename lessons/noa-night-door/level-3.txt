Noa and the Night Door

Noa sees a black door in the library.

Yuto touches it. The door is cold.

Iori reads a card on the door. "Maybe open. Maybe not."

"That is not helpful," Yuto says.

Noa looks at the card again. Under the words is a small moon.

"Maybe the door opens only at night," she says.

They wait until the hall is dark. The moon comes through the window. The black door becomes blue.

Behind it is not a room. It is a small garden with one sleeping star.

The star says, "Too bright."

Noa closes the curtain. The garden becomes quiet.

The star smiles and gives them a silver bookmark.

Iori reads it. "Maybe not all closed doors are saying no."
