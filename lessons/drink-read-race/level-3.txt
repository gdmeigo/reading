The Drink and Read Race

Taro makes a new club game.

"Drink water, read one line, and run to the board," he says.

Ken drinks water. Mika reads a line. Taro runs to the board before them.

"That is not fair," Mika says. "You did not read."

Taro opens the book. "I read the title."

Ken watches him. The title is very short: "Go!"

They play again. This time everyone must read three lines.

Taro reads slowly. Ken and Mika run first.

Taro laughs. "Reading is harder than running."

Mika writes on the board: "Read first. Run second."

At lunch, three more students join the game.

One student reads very fast, but runs the wrong way.

Taro points at the board. "Read carefully, too."
