The Drink and Read Race

Ren makes a new club game.

"Drink water, read one line, and run to the board," he says.

Kaito drinks water. Saki reads a line. Ren runs to the board before them.

"That is not fair," Saki says. "You did not read."

Ren opens the book. "I read the title."

Kaito watches him. The title is very short: "Go!"

They play again. This time everyone must read three lines.

Ren reads slowly. Kaito and Saki run first.

Ren laughs. "Reading is harder than running."

Saki writes on the board: "Read first. Run second."

At lunch, three more students join the game.

One student reads very fast, but runs the wrong way.

Ren points at the board. "Read carefully, too."
