The Moon Cup

Yui visited Grandma's house with Ken.
There was a blue cup on the shelf.
Grandma said, "Do not drink from that cup."

Yui did not drink.
Ken did not drink.
But they put water in the cup.

The water went up like smoke.
It was cold.
Then a small moon came out of the cup.

"I need my shadow," it said.

"Why do you need it?" asked Ken.
"I know how to help," said Yui.

Ken looked under the shelf.
Yui looked in the box.
They saw nothing.

Then Ken saw a drum by the door.
It was round like the moon.
On the back of it, there was a black circle.

"Maybe this is the shadow," said Yui.

The black circle moved.
