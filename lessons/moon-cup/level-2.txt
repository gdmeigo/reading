The Moon Cup

Yui is at Grandma's house.
There is a blue cup on the shelf.
The cup is small, but it is not like the other cups.

"Do not use that cup," says Grandma.

Ken comes after dinner.
Yui shows him the cup.

"What is in it?" asks Ken.
"Nothing," says Yui.

Then she puts water in the cup.
The water goes up.
A small moon is in the water.

Ken steps back.
Yui does not step back.

The moon says, "I need my shadow."
