The Moon Cup

Yui visited Grandma's house during the summer festival.
On the shelf, there was a blue cup between two white cups.

"Do not drink from the blue cup after sunset," said Grandma.

After dinner, Ken came with festival tickets.
Yui showed him the cup.
When she looked through it, the room was upside down.

"Maybe it is old glass," said Ken.

Yui put water in the cup.
The water rose like cold steam.
A small moon appeared above it.

"I am looking for my shadow," said the moon.

Ken and Yui looked around the house.
They found boxes, bags, and old books, but there was no shadow.

Then Yui saw the festival drum.
It was full and round, but inside it had nothing.
On the back was a black circle.

Grandma came in and smiled.
"You found it," she said.
