X Marks the Lunch Box

Kaito opens his lunch box and sees a big X on the rice.

"This is more than lunch," Ren says.

Miki finds a small paper under the box. It says, "Follow the X."

Saki points at the yard. There is another X made with two sticks.

The group follows the marks to the flower bed. Under a stone is a note.

"Give water to the small one."

Kaito sees a tiny plant. It is dry.

Ren gets water. Nana holds the plant straight. Saki covers it from the sun.

At lunch, Kaito opens the box again. Under the rice is one more note.

"Thank you for finding my plant."

Miki laughs. "This was more than lunch. It was a garden map."
