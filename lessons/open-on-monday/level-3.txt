Open on Monday

Taro sees a box on the teacher's desk.

The box says, "Open on Monday."

"Today is Monday," Taro says.

Hana points to another note. It says, "Don't be fast."

Jiro reads a third note. "Don't be slow."

Taro looks tired. "What should I be?"

Jaio comes in and says, "Be strong. Open it now."

Suneta nods. "Strong and fast."

Taro opens the box very carefully. Inside is another box.

It says, "Open after reading all notes."

Hana finds the last note under the desk. It says, "Don't be alone."

Everyone opens the small box together.

Inside are cookies and one paper crown.

Jiro puts the crown on the box. "The box is the teacher."
