Open on Monday

Taro sees a box on the teacher's desk.

The box says, "Open on Monday."

"Today is Monday," Taro says.

Hana points to another note. It says, "Don't be fast."

Jiro reads a third note. "Don't be slow."

Taro looks tired. "What should I be?"

Jaio comes in and says, "Be strong. Open it now."

Suneta nods. "Strong and fast."

Taro opens the box very carefully.

Inside is a big sign. It says, "OPEN."

Hana looks at the first note again. "Maybe it says, 'Put OPEN on Monday.'"

"Then what should we do?" asks Taro.

Jiro points to the board. On the board are five words: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday.

Hana puts the OPEN sign on Monday.

Jaio smiles. "Great. Tuesday is not open. We should go home tomorrow."

Suneta nods. "Strong idea."

Mr. Tanaka comes in and reads the back of the sign.

It says, "If Jaio says go home, the joke is working."

Everyone looks at Jaio.

Jaio closes the box. "This box knows too much."
