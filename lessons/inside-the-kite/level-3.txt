Inside the Kite

Daichi finds a kite by the school gate.

It is old, but the paper is clean. Emi sees small words on one side.

"Who wrote this?" she asks.

Daichi reads the words. "Please fly once more."

Yuna comes to the gate. She looks surprised.

"That is my grandfather's kite," she says. "He cannot run now, so he put it here."

Daichi looks at the sky. There is a soft wind.

Aya brings string from the club room. Emi holds the kite. Daichi runs across the ground.

The kite goes up. A small paper falls from inside it.

Yuna reads it. "If it flies, smile."

She smiles first. Then everyone smiles.

After school, Yuna writes a new note and puts it inside the kite: It flew.
