The Window Note

After class, Ken sees words on the window.

"Write here," the words say.

Ken looks at Yui. "Who can write on a window?"

Yui puts her hand on the glass. The words are not on the inside. They are on the outside.

Ken opens the window. A small notebook falls in.

On the first page, someone writes, "Please find my pencil."

Yui sees a pencil by the flower pot. Ken takes it.

The next page says, "Thank you."

"How did it write that?" Ken asks.

Then a little girl comes to the window. It is Emi from the next class.

"I wrote it before lunch," she says. "But the wind closed the window."

Ken gives her the pencil. Emi smiles. "Next time, I will use the door."
