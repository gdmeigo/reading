The Window Note

After class, Ken sees words on the window.

"Write here," the words say.

Ken looks at Mika. "Who can write on a window?"

Mika puts her hand on the glass. The words are not on the inside. They are on the outside.

Ken opens the window. A small notebook falls in.

On the first page, someone writes, "Please find my pencil."

Mika sees a pencil by the flower pot. Ken takes it.

The next page says, "Thank you."

"How did it write that?" Ken asks.

Then a little girl comes to the window. It is Mika from the next class.

"I wrote it before lunch," she says. "But the wind closed the window."

Ken gives her the pencil. Mika smiles. "Next time, I will use the door."
