Rina's Brother

Rina's brother is small. His name is Sota.

Rina has a picture for school. It is a picture of her family: her mother, her father, her sister, and Sota.

But Sota takes the picture. He runs to the door.

"That is my homework," Rina says.

Sota points at the picture. "Me," he says.

Rina looks again. In the picture, Sota's face has no smile. He is very serious.

"Oh," Rina says. "You want a happy face."

She draws a small smile on Sota's face in the picture. Sota smiles, too.

At school, Rina shows the picture.

"This is my family," she says. "And this is my brother's best smile."

Her teacher smiles. "That is a good picture."

Rina thinks, "Thank you, Sota."
