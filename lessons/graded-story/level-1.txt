The Little Door

Ken has a small key.
Yui sees the key.
The key is not new.
It is old.

Ken puts the key on the desk.
Yui sees a small door under the desk.
The door is shut.

Ken puts the key in the door.
The door opens.
Ken and Yui see a red bag.
The bag is small.

Something is in the bag.
The bag moves.
Ken looks at Yui.
Yui looks at Ken.

"What is in the bag?"
