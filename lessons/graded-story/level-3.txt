The Little Door, Part 1

Ken came to school with a small key.
He put the key on his desk.
Mika came to Ken's desk.
She saw the key.

"What is that?" she said.
"It is a key," said Ken.
"But it is not my key."

Then Mika saw a small door under Ken's desk.
"Can you see the door?" she asked.
The door was shut.
Ken put the key in the door.
The door opened.

Ken and Mika saw a red bag.
The bag was in the small door.
Ken took the bag out.

The bag moved.
Ken did not open it.
Mika did not open it.

Then the bag moved again.
Ken said, "I think something is in this bag."
