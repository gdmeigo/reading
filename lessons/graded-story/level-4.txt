The Little Door

Ken came into the classroom before class.
He had an old key in his hand.
He did not know the key.
He found it in his shoe box.

Yui came to his desk.
"What do you have?" she asked.
"An old key," said Ken.
"It was in my shoe box."

Yui looked under Ken's desk.
There was a very small door.
It was behind a book.
Ken and Yui did not see the door yesterday.

Ken put the key in the door.
The door opened.
In the door they saw a red bag.
Ken took it out and put it on the desk.

The bag moved.
Ken and Yui moved back.
"Open it," said Yui.
"No," said Ken. "You open it."

The bag moved again.
Then they heard a small sound.
It was not a bird.
It was not a cat.
It was a small toy dog.
The toy dog was in the red bag.

Ken took the toy dog out.
The toy dog had a gold key.

Yui looked at the small door.
"Try it," she said.

Ken put the gold key in the door.
The small door opened again.
There was a small box.
There was a card in it.
The card said, "Happy birthday, Ken."

Ken looked at Yui.
Yui smiled.
Then the teacher came in with a cake.

"Happy birthday!" said Yui and the teacher.
Ken was very happy.
