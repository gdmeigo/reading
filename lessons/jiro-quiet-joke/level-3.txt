Jiro's Quiet Joke

Taro wants to make the class laugh.

He puts a paper fish on his back and waits.

No one laughs.

Hana looks at him. "You should not wait. You should walk."

Taro walks. Still, no one laughs.

Jiro gives Taro a mirror.

"Show me the joke," Jiro says.

Taro looks in the mirror and sees the fish. On the fish are the words, "Please laugh quietly."

Taro whispers, "Ha ha."

Hana whispers, "Ha ha."

Soon the whole class is laughing, but the room is very quiet.

Mr. Tanaka comes in. "Why is everyone smiling?"

Jiro gives him the mirror.

Mr. Tanaka reads the fish and whispers, "Ha ha."

Taro bows. "This is my best quiet joke."
