Quiet Question

Ken finds one question on the board.

"Who should open the small box?"

Mika says, "A question should have a reason."

Riku says, "A box should have candy."

The box is on the teacher's desk. It has no lock, but nobody opens it.

Ken looks at the board again. Under the question is a tiny mark.

Mika sees the same mark on Riku's notebook.

Riku looks surprised. "I did not write it today."

Mr. Sato comes in. "You wrote it last year," he says. "You wanted to ask your future class one brave question."

Riku opens the box. Inside is his old note.

It says, "Should we be kinder than yesterday?"

The room becomes quiet.

Ken says, "Yes."
