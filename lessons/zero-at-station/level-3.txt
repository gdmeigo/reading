Zero at the Station

Sora and Mina stand at the old station.

The clock says zero.

"That is not a time," Mina says.

Leo looks at the ticket machine. It has one button: START.

"Do we have to press it?" he asks.

Aiko reads the sign. "We have to wait first."

The three students wait. The train line is quiet. Then the clock changes from zero to one.

A small train of light comes down the line. It stops in front of them, but the doors do not open.

Sora sees words on the window: "Where do you want to begin?"

Mina says, "At school."

The train light becomes a small map. It shows the way to the science room.

Aiko smiles. "Zero was not the end. It was the start."
