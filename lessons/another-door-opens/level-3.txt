Another Door Opens

Ken sees a small door behind the black board.

"Was it opened by someone?" Mika asks.

Riku looks at the door. "Maybe it opened itself. I do not like that."

Ken pulls the door. It is not a room. It is a box in the wall. Inside is a white card.

The card says, "Open when the class is quiet."

The three students wait. The hall is noisy. The room is noisy. Then the bell rings, and everyone stops.

Ken opens the card. It says, "Thank you. The quiet class gets one extra minute outside."

Mr. Sato comes in and smiles.

"The door was made by last year's class," he says. "They wanted to give you a quiet game."

Riku stands up. "I like this door now."

Mika writes on the board: Another door opens when a class can listen.
