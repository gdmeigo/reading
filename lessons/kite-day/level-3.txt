The Paper Rocket

Daichi came to school with a paper rocket.
Today was show day.
He put the rocket on his desk.

Aya came to Daichi's desk.
"Is that your rocket?" she asked.
"Yes," said Daichi. "It can go up."

Daichi put the rocket on a pump.
He pushed the pump.
The rocket went up a little.
Then it came down.

Some boys laughed.
Daichi did not laugh.

Aya looked at the rocket.
There was a small hole in it.
She put tape on the hole.

Daichi pushed the pump again.
The rocket went up.
It went over the desks.

This time, everyone laughed in a happy way.
