The Paper Rocket

Ken came into the classroom with a paper rocket.
It was show day, and Ken was first.
He wanted the rocket to go over the desks.

Yui sat in the front row.
"Good luck," she said.

Ken put the rocket on a small pump.
He pushed the pump.
The rocket went up, but only a little.
Then it fell onto the floor.

The room became quiet.
Ken picked up the rocket.
"It worked at home," he said.

Yui looked at the rocket carefully.
There was a small hole near the bottom.
"Air is going out," she said.

Ken had no tape.
Yui had tape in her pencil case.
She put tape on the hole.

Ken tried again.
This time, the rocket went up and over the first desk.
Then it came down on Mr. Sato's book.

Mr. Sato looked at the rocket.
Then he smiled.
"Good landing," he said.

The class clapped.
Ken smiled, too.
