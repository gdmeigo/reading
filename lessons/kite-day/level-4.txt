The Paper Rocket

Daichi came into the classroom with a paper rocket.
It was show day, and Daichi was first.
He wanted the rocket to go over the desks.

Aya sat in the front row.
"Good luck," she said.

Daichi put the rocket on a small pump.
He pushed the pump.
The rocket went up, but only a little.
Then it fell onto the floor.

The room became quiet.
Daichi picked up the rocket.
"It worked at home," he said.

Aya looked at the rocket carefully.
There was a small hole near the bottom.
"Air is going out," she said.

Daichi had no tape.
Aya had tape in her pencil case.
She put tape on the hole.

Daichi tried again.
This time, the rocket went up and over the first desk.
Then it came down on Mr. Sato's book.

Mr. Sato looked at the rocket.
Then he smiled.
"Good landing," he said.

The class clapped.
Daichi smiled, too.
