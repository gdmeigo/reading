The Paper Rocket

Daichi has a paper rocket.
Aya sees the rocket.
It is big.
It is red.

Daichi puts it on the desk.
"It can go up," he says.

The rocket does not go up.
It falls.

Daichi is sad.
Aya sees a small hole.
She puts tape on the hole.

Daichi tries again.
The rocket goes up.

The class says, "Wow!"
Daichi smiles.
