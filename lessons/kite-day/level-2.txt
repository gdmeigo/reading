The Paper Rocket

Ken came to school with a paper rocket.
Today was show day.
He put the rocket on his desk.

Yui came to Ken's desk.
"Is that your rocket?" she asked.
"Yes," said Ken. "It can go up."

Ken put the rocket on a pump.
He pushed the pump.
The rocket went up a little.
Then it came down.

Some boys laughed.
Ken did not laugh.

Yui looked at the rocket.
There was a small hole in it.
She put tape on the hole.

Ken pushed the pump again.
The rocket went up.
It went over the desks.

This time, everyone laughed in a happy way.
