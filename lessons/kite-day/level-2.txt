The Paper Rocket

Ren came to school with a paper rocket.
Today was show day.
He put the rocket on his desk.

Miki came to Ren's desk.
"Is that your rocket?" she asked.
"Yes," said Ren. "It can go up."

Ren put the rocket on a pump.
He pushed the pump.
The rocket went up a little.
Then it came down.

Some boys laughed.
Ren did not laugh.

Miki looked at the rocket.
There was a small hole in it.
She put tape on the hole.

Ren pushed the pump again.
The rocket went up.
It went over the desks.

This time, everyone laughed in a happy way.
