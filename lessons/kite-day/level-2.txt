The Paper Rocket

Ren has a paper rocket.
Miki sees the rocket.
It is big.
It is red.

Ren puts it on the desk.
"It can go up," he says.

The rocket does not go up.
It falls.

Ren is sad.
Miki sees a small hole.
She puts tape on the hole.

Ren tries again.
The rocket goes up.

The class says, "Wow!"
Ren smiles.
