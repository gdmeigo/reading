The Paper Rocket

Ken has a paper rocket.
Yui sees the rocket.
It is big.
It is red.

Ken puts it on the desk.
"It can go up," he says.

The rocket does not go up.
It falls.

Ken is sad.
Yui sees a small hole.
She puts tape on the hole.

Ken tries again.
The rocket goes up.

The class says, "Wow!"
Ken smiles.
