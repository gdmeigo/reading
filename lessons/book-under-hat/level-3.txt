The Book Under the Hat

"This book is old," Taro says.

"This book is not old," Hana says. "It is a new book."

The book is on the desk. Then the light goes off. When the light is on again, the book is not on the desk.

"Where is it?" Taro asks.

Hana looks at a big black hat on the seat. The hat is too tall.

"This hat is strange," she says.

Taro lifts the hat. The book is under it. A small card is on the book.

The card says, "Read page one."

Taro reads page one. It says, "Look under the hat."

Hana laughs. "The book is a joke book."

Taro opens it again. On page two it says, "Now look under your seat."

Taro looks. There is a cookie.

"This book is good," he says.
