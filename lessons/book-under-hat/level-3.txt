The Book Under the Hat

"This book is old," Ken says.

"This book is not old," Hana says. "It is a new book."

The book is on the desk. Then the light goes off. When the light is on again, the book is not on the desk.

"Where is it?" Ken asks.

Hana looks at a big black hat on the chair. The hat is too tall.

"This hat is strange," she says.

Ken lifts the hat. The book is under it. A small card is on the book.

The card says, "Read page one."

Ken reads page one. It says, "Look under the hat."

Hana laughs. "The book is a joke book."

Ken opens it again. On page two it says, "Now look under your chair."

Ken looks. There is a cookie.

"This book is good," he says.
