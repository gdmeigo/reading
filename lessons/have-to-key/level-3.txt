The Have-To Key

The art room door is locked. The pictures for the festival are inside.

"We have to find the key," Kaito says.

Saki looks in the office. Ren looks under the mat. There is no key.

Then Nana points at a clay cat near the door. A key is around its neck.

"We have to ask the cat?" Ren says.

Kaito reads the small card on the cat. "Say thank you."

Saki says, "Thank you, cat."

Kaito takes the key and opens the door.

Inside, their pictures are safe.

Ren puts the key back on the cat. "We have to be polite," he says.

The next morning, someone puts a small paper fish by the cat.

Kaito reads the card. "Thank you, key cat."

Saki smiles. The cat looks proud.
