The Have-To Key

The art room door is locked. The pictures for the festival are inside.

"We have to find the key," Ken says.

Mika looks in the office. Taro looks under the mat. There is no key.

Then Yui points at a clay cat near the door. A key is around its neck.

"We have to ask the cat?" Taro says.

Ken reads the small card on the cat. "Say thank you."

Mika says, "Thank you, cat."

Ken takes the key and opens the door.

Inside, their pictures are safe.

Taro puts the key back on the cat. "We have to be polite," he says.

The next morning, someone puts a small paper fish by the cat.

Ken reads the card. "Thank you, key cat."

Mika smiles. The cat looks proud.
