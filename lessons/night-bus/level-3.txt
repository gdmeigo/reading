The Last Bus

Riku and Mika came to the bus stop after school.
An old woman was sitting on the bench.
She had a small bag.

The bus came.
The woman looked at the bus.
But she did not get on.

Riku saw a card on the bench.
It said, "Hospital."

Mika gave the card to the woman.
"Is this yours?" she asked.

The woman looked at the card.
"Yes," she said.
"I need this bus."

Riku and Mika helped her get on.
The woman smiled from the window.
