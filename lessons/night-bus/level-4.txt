The Last Bus

Riku and Mika came to the bus stop after school.
The sky was getting dark.
An old woman was sitting on the bench.
She had a small bag and a white card.

The last bus came.
The woman stood up.
Then she sat down again.

"Is something wrong?" asked Mika.
The woman looked in her bag.
"My card is gone," she said.

Riku looked under the bench.
There was a white card.
It said, "City Hospital."

Mika gave it to the woman.
The woman looked very happy.
"My husband is in the hospital," she said.
"I need this bus."

Riku helped her with the bag.
Mika told the driver to wait.

The woman got on the bus.
Before the bus went away, she waved from the window.

The next day, Riku and Mika saw the woman again.
This time, she was with her husband.
They both said, "Thank you."
