The Poster Was Moved

The festival poster was moved.

Yesterday it was by the library. Today it is by the gym.

"It was moved by someone," Ken says.

Mika sees a small tape mark on the wall. Taro sees a blue thread on the poster.

They follow the blue thread to the gym. Yui is there with a box of tape.

"I moved it," she says. "The wind came in by the library, and the poster fell."

Ken looks at the gym wall. Many students can see the poster here.

"Good idea," Mika says.

Taro writes a small note: "This poster was moved by Yui."

Yui laughs. "Now everyone knows."

At lunch, many students stop by the gym.

"The poster was moved, but the festival was saved," Ken says.

Yui smiles at the big wall.
