The Poster Was Moved

The festival poster was moved.

Yesterday it was by the library. Today it is by the gym.

"It was moved by someone," Kaito says.

Saki sees a small tape mark on the wall. Ren sees a blue thread on the poster.

They follow the blue thread to the gym. Nana is there with a box of tape.

"I moved it," she says. "The wind came in by the library, and the poster fell."

Kaito looks at the gym wall. Many students can see the poster here.

"Good idea," Saki says.

Ren writes a small note: "This poster was moved by Nana."

Nana laughs. "Now everyone knows."

At lunch, many students stop by the gym.

"The poster was moved, but the festival was saved," Kaito says.

Nana smiles at the big wall.
