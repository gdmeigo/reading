The Doll's Foot

Mika and Ken are in the club room.

A doll is on the desk. Its arm is in a cup. Its foot is on a book. Its leg is in a red box.

Mika took the arm and put it on the doll. Ken took the leg and put it on the doll.

The doll was funny. Its arm was on the left. Its leg was on the right. The foot was off.

On the foot was a small star. On the book was a small star, too.

Ken put the foot on the star in the book. In the book was a card.

The card was Ken's. The candy was Mika's.

Mika took the candy. Ken took the doll.

Its arm, its foot, and its leg were in the right place.

Mika put the candy in the cup. Ken put the doll on the book.

The small mystery was in the right place, too.
