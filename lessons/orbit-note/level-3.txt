The Orbit Note

Mina finds a black notebook in the library.
On the first page, someone writes, "Look up."

Mina looks up.
A small paper spaceship is moving near the ceiling.

Sora tries to catch it, but the ship turns left.
Mina writes a note: "Please stop."

The ship comes down and lands on the note.

Inside the ship is a tiny screen.
It shows the school from above.

"It is in orbit," Sora says.

The screen blinks.
Then new words appear: "Write one kind word."

Mina writes, "Welcome."

The paper spaceship opens its door.
A small light comes out and flies around the room.

Mina smiles. "Maybe it needed a place to land."
