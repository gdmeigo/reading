The Travel Ticket

Ken finds a train ticket near the school gate.

"Someone will travel today," Mika says.

They visit the office, but no one is there. They listen at the hall door. A boy is talking with his mother on the phone.

"I feel bad," the boy says. "I lost it."

Ken shows him the ticket.

The boy's face changes. "That is mine! I will visit my grandfather."

Mika smiles. "Then go."

The boy runs to the station. Ken and Mika stand at the gate and wave.

After school, a small box is on Ken's desk. Inside are two rice crackers and a note.

"Thank you. My grandfather was very happy."

Ken feels warm. One small ticket made a long happy trip.
