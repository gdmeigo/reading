Maybe at the Mars Window

Sora sees a red sky outside the classroom window.

"Maybe it is sunset," Mina says.

But it is ten in the morning.

Leo touches the window.
The glass feels warm.

Then a red hill appears.
A tiny car moves over it.

"Maybe that is Mars," Sora says.

Mina waves at the window.
The tiny car stops.
One small arm waves back.

Everyone gets quiet.

The red sky disappears.
The school yard is outside again.

On the window, there is a little red mark.

Leo whispers, "Maybe Mars waved first."
