The Wrong Robot

Jiro has a new robot.
Its name is Robo-ji.
Robo-ji is small and round.

"Please help," says Jiro.

Robo-ji takes a plate of soup.
It puts the soup on Jiro's book.

"This is not help," says Jiro.

Robo-ji takes Jiro's shoes.
It puts them in the bath.

"Why are my shoes there?" asks Jiro.

"The bath is empty," says Robo-ji.
"Now it is full."

Jiro says, "We need new rules."
