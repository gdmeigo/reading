The Wrong Robot

Sota has a new robot.
Its name is Robo-ji.
Robo-ji is small and round.

"Please help," says Sota.

Robo-ji takes a plate of soup.
It puts the soup on Sota's book.

"This is not help," says Sota.

Robo-ji takes Sota's shoes.
It puts them in the bath.

"Why are my shoes there?" asks Sota.

"The bath is empty," says Robo-ji.
"Now it is full."

Sota says, "We need new rules."
