The Star Bus

Ken and Mina are at an old bus stop.
It is morning, but the sky is dark.

A silver bus comes.
There is a blue star on the door.

"What is this bus?" asks Mina.
"I do not know," says Ken.

They go in.
The bus is quiet.
There is a small bag on one seat.
The bag is not Ken's bag.
It is not Mina's bag.

The bag moves again.

Ken sees a light in it.
Mina sees it, too.

"Maybe this bus is not for school," she says.
