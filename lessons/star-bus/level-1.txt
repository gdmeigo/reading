The Star Bus

Ken sees a bus.
The bus is silver.
It is here.

Mina sees the bus, too.
"Is this our bus?" she says.

Ken sees a star on the door.
The star is small.
It is blue.

The bus opens.
Ken and Mina go in.

There is a bag on the seat.
The bag moves.

Ken says, "Oh."
Mina says, "Maybe it is a star."
