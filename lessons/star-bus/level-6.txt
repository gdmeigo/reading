The Star Bus

Sora and Mina came to the old bus stop at 5:35 in the morning. They wanted to watch the last star shower of the year, but the sky was covered with clouds. Mina was not sure about Sora's plan.

"Why are we waiting here?" she asked. "This bus stop is not used now."

Sora pointed at the sign. LAST BUS: 5:40. Under the words, there was a small blue star.

At 5:39, a silver bus came out of the fog. It made no sound, and its windows were full of pale light. The driver was an old woman with blue gloves.

"Are you against the trip?" she asked.

"We don't know the trip," said Sora.

"Then you can join it," said the driver.

The bus went up, but the wheels stayed on the road. The town became small below them. At the back of the bus, Mina found a black bag with one word on it: HOME.

The bag moved.

"Do not open it," said the driver.

The bag opened by itself. A tiny star came out, weak and quiet. In the window, Sora saw a picture of their school. The old music room was dark.

"It is looking for a place that stopped shining," Mina said.

When the bus came back, Sora and Mina went to the music room and set up one small lamp. That night, one star moved above the school, as if it was saying thank you.
