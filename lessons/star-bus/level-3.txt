The Star Bus

Sora came to the bus stop with Mina.
They wanted to see the star shower.
No bus was there at first.

Then a silver bus came out of the fog.
It had a blue star on the door.

"Did you see that?" said Mina.
"Yes," said Sora. "But I did not hear it."

Mina looked at the door. "Can this bus fly?" she asked.
Sora looked at the road. "Can a bus do that?"

They got on the bus.
There was a black bag on the last seat.
The bag moved once.

Sora did not open it.
Mina did not open it.

Then the bus went up.
The road was under them.
The town was small.

Mina looked out of the window.
"Sora," she said, "this is not a bus."

The bag opened.
A small star was in it.
