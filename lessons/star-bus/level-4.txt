The Star Bus

Sora and Mina waited at the old bus stop before sunrise.
They wanted to watch the star shower, but there were no stars in the sky.

Then a silver bus came through the fog.
There was a blue star on its door.
The driver was an old woman with bright gloves.

"Where does this bus go?" asked Sora.
"Up," said the driver.

Mina wanted to get off, but the door shut.
The bus went up slowly.
There were children in the other seats.
One child had a light in a glass box.

At the back of the bus, there was a black bag.
Sora saw it move.
Mina saw it move, too.

"Don't open it," said the driver.

The bag opened by itself.
Inside it was a small star which was not shining.

The driver said, "It is looking for a place which needs light."

Sora thought about the old music room at school.
It was always dark.
