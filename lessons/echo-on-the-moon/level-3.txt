Echo on the Moon

Sora and Mina watch the moon from the science room.

A small radio says, "Hello."

Mina turns it off. The radio says, "Hello," again.

"Why does it speak?" Sora asks.

Aiko looks at the screen. "It is not speaking. It is an echo because our old message came back."

Leo reads the note by the radio. It says, "Send one word to the moon."

Sora remembers. Last month, the club sent the word "home." The word went up, and nobody heard it again.

Now the radio says, "Home."

Mina smiles. "The moon is slow, but it is polite."

Sora writes a new word: "Thanks."

The radio is quiet.

Then it says, "Wait."

Leo jumps back. "The moon wants a conversation."
