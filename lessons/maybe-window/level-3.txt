Maybe at the Window

Saki sees a white hand at the music room window.

"Maybe it is a ghost," Ren says.

"Maybe it is a teacher," Kaito says.

They walk to the music room. The room is empty. The window is closed.

Then the white hand moves again.

Saki opens the curtain. It is not a hand. It is a white glove on a long stick.

Nana is outside. "Sorry," she says. "My glove fell on the tree. I used this stick."

Ren looks at the glove. "Maybe it is still a little ghost."

Nana puts it on and waves.

Everyone laughs. The ghost says hello and goes home.

The next day, Ren puts a small sign on the window.

It says, "Maybe a glove. Maybe not."

Saki reads it and laughs again.
