The Banana Principal

Riku came to school with a banana in his bag.
It was for lunch.
But when he opened his bag, the banana was not there.

"What's wrong?" asked Emi.

"My banana is gone," said Riku.

Then they heard a voice from the hall.
"Don't be late!"

It was the principal.
But he had something yellow on his head.

Emi looked at Riku.
Riku looked at Emi.

"Is that your banana?" she asked.

"Maybe," said Riku.

The principal came into the classroom.
He did not know about the banana.
He said, "How is everyone today?"

"Come here, please," said Emi.

Nobody answered.
Everyone looked at his head.

Riku stood up.
"Let's help him," he said.

Emi took a small mirror from her bag.
The principal saw the banana on his head.

"Oh," he said. "I thought it was my yellow hat."

The class laughed.
The principal laughed, too.

Then Riku saw his real banana on his desk.
There was nothing on it.

Emi smiled.
"Now you have a banana and a story."
