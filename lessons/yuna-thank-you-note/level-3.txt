Yuna's Thank-You Note

Yuna's brother is in the first grade.

He does not like the big school gate.

"It is too tall," he says.

Yuna walks with him every morning. One day, Daichi and Emi are by the gate. They say, "Good morning," in soft voices.

Yuna's brother looks up. "The gate is not angry today."

After class, Yuna writes a note.

It says, "Thank you for making the gate small."

Daichi reads it and looks confused. "We did not make it small."

Emi smiles. "Maybe we did."

The next morning, Yuna's brother walks through the gate by himself.

He turns back and waves.

Yuna puts another note in her pocket. This one says, "He did it."
