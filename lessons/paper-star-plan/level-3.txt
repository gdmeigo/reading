Paper Star Plan

Yui makes a paper star for the festival.

Haru looks at it. "Can it fly?"

"It is paper," Mio says. "But ask it."

Yui holds the star near the window. "Can you fly?"

The star shakes once.

Haru steps back. "That means yes, I think."

The star flies across the room and stops above a dark lantern.

Mio reads the words on the lantern. "I cannot shine alone."

Yui puts the paper star inside it. The lantern becomes warm and bright.

Haru smiles. "So the plan was not to make a star fly."

Yui nods. "It was to help a lantern ask."
