The Should Sign

There is a new sign in the hall.

"You should walk," it says.

Taro walks very slowly. Then he stops.

"You should not stop," Mika says.

Ken sees another sign. "You should be kind."

Taro gives Ken his bag. "I am kind."

"You should carry your own bag," Ken says.

At the end of the hall, Mr. Sato is putting up one more sign.

It says, "You should read the whole sign."

Under the words, there is a picture: "Walk to the library. The book fair is open."

Taro runs one step, then stops. "I should walk."

Mika smiles. "Good. And we should buy a funny book."

Ken buys a mystery book. Taro buys a book about signs.

"Why signs?" Ken asks.

Taro grins. "I should study my enemy."
