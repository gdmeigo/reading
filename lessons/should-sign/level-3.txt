The Should Sign

There is a new sign in the hall.

"You should walk," it says.

Ren walks very slowly. Then he stops.

"You should not stop," Saki says.

Kaito sees another sign. "You should be kind."

Ren gives Kaito his bag. "I am kind."

"You should carry your own bag," Kaito says.

At the end of the hall, Mr. Sato is putting up one more sign.

It says, "You should read the whole sign."

Under the words, there is a picture: "Walk to the library. The book fair is open."

Ren runs one step, then stops. "I should walk."

Saki smiles. "Good. And we should buy a funny book."

Kaito buys a mystery book. Ren buys a book about signs.

"Why signs?" Kaito asks.

Ren grins. "I should study my enemy."
