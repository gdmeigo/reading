Green Glass Signal

Sora puts a green glass by the window.

Mina puts a pen in a cup of water. The pen looks short in the water.

"These pens are normal," she says. "The water is the trick."

Then the green glass shines on the wall. It makes three small lines.

Leo looks at the lines. "Are they a signal?"

Aiko moves the glass. The lines move, too.

Sora sees the moon outside. "The moonlight is going through the glass and the water."

Mina puts the pen beside the glass. The line becomes an arrow.

The arrow points to the science room door.

On the door is a note: "Good. You found the light path."

Leo smiles. "The pen was not the message. It was the pointer."
