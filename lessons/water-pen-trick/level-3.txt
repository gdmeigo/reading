The Water Pen Trick

These pens are long. Ken puts them by a glass of water.

"Look," Mika says. She puts one pen in the water.

Ken sees the pen in the glass. It looks short.

"These pens are not the same," he says.

Mika takes the pen out. It is long again.

Ken looks at the water. "Is it a magic glass?"

Mika shakes her head. "No. The water and the glass make it look different."

Ken puts two pens in the water. They look funny. One looks bent, and one looks short.

Riku comes in. "My pen is broken!"

Ken and Mika laugh. "No, it is only in the water."

Riku takes it out. The pen is fine.

"Good," he says. "Then my homework is not broken, either."
