Under the Red Desk

There is one red desk in the classroom.

Nobody uses it because it makes a sound.

Tap. Tap. Tap.

Riku says, "We should leave it alone."

Ken says, "We should look under it."

Mika puts paper on the floor and looks under the desk. A small pencil is tied to a string. When the wind comes in, the pencil hits the wood.

"So it is not a ghost," Riku says.

Ken follows the string. It goes to a box under the teacher's table.

Inside the box is a note: "Find the quiet pencil."

Mr. Sato smiles from the door.

Mika puts the pencil in the box. The desk becomes quiet.

Riku sits at it. "Now this is the best seat."
