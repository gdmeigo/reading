The Blue Lunch Box

Ken came to school before class.
He saw a blue lunch box on the bench by the shoe boxes.
There was no name on it.

Yui came to the bench.
"Is it yours?" she asked.
"No," said Ken. "My lunch box is green."

Ken opened the blue lunch box.
There was no lunch in it.
There was a small note and a white ball.

Yui read the note.
"Please help my friend. She is cold."

The white ball moved.
Ken moved back.
Yui looked at it carefully.
"It is not a ball," she said.
"It is a small bird."

The bird was very small.
It did not fly.
Ken put his hand near the bird.
The bird came into his hand.

Then a girl came to the shoe boxes.
She was looking for something.
She looked at the bench.
She looked at Ken's hand.

"My bird!" she said.

Ken gave the bird to the girl.
The girl smiled.
"Thank you," she said.

The blue lunch box was hers.
It was not a lunch box today.
It was a small warm house.
