The Blue Lunch Box

Daichi came to school early.
He saw a blue lunch box on the bench.
It was not his lunch box.

Emi came to the bench.
"Is that your lunch box?" she asked.
"No," said Daichi. "I don't know it."

Daichi opened the lunch box.
There was no rice in it.
There was no egg in it.
There was only a small note.

Emi took the note.
The note said, "Please help my friend."

Then the lunch box moved.
Daichi shut it.
Emi said, "Don't shut it."

Daichi opened it again.
There was a small white ball in it.
The ball moved.

"It is not a ball," said Emi.
