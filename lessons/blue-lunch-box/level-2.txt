The Blue Lunch Box

Daichi sees a blue lunch box.
It is on the bench.
It is not Daichi's lunch box.

Emi sees the lunch box, too.
"Is it your lunch box?" she asks.
"No," says Daichi.

Daichi opens the lunch box.
There is no lunch in it.
There is a small note.

The note says, "Please help."
Daichi looks at Emi.
Emi looks at Daichi.

Then the lunch box moves.
