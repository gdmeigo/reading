Kaito's Key Map

Kaito finds a key on the gym floor.

Miki finds a map under it. The map has three X marks.

"How to use this key?" Ren asks.

Saki reads the small words. "Do not open a door first. Open a question."

Kaito looks at the first X. It is by the music room. There is no door there, only a box.

Inside the box is a question: "Where do lost balls go?"

Ren runs to the yard. A bag of old balls is by the fence. In the bag is another note.

The last X is at the gym.

Kaito opens the small room with the key. Inside are new balls for the class.

Miki smiles. "The key did not open a secret. It opened practice."
